My top 3 movies of all time

Prison Break

Prison Break is a TV series about two brothers, Michael Scofield and Lincoln Burrows. Lincoln is wrongfully convicted of murdering the Vice President's brother and sentenced to death. Michael, a structural engineer, gets himself incarcerated in the same prison to break his brother out. The series follows Michael's intricate plan, his interactions with other inmates and prison staff, and the brothers' fight to evade capture and expose the conspiracy that framed Lincoln

Weak Hero

"Weak Hero Class 1" is a South Korean drama about a top student named Yeon Si-eun who, despite his seemingly frail appearance, uses his intelligence and strategic thinking to fight against escalating school violence
